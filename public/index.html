Core Capabilities

Built for intelligent network defence

SVM Classification Engine
Utilises Support Vector Machines with RBF, linear and polynomial kernels to maximise the margin between normal and malicious traffic classes.
Live Traffic Monitoring
Real-time packet analysis streams each connection through the model, flagging and blocking threats the instant they appear on the network.
Dataset Upload & Analysis
Drag and drop your own .csv or .pcap file. The system extracts features automatically and returns per-packet predictions with confidence scores.
Model Evaluation
Interactive dashboards display Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, ROC curves, and a full confusion matrix for model transparency.
Reports & Audit Logs
Full scan history with date, IP and attack-type filters. Export complete audit reports as PDF or CSV for academic documentation.
Benchmark Datasets
Trained and validated on NSL-KDD, UNSW-NB15, and CICIDS2017/2018 — industry-standard datasets covering DoS, Probe, U2R and R2L attacks.
